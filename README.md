 As a part of my UI-UX Workshop, i created CampusConnect- a mobile app prototype designed to help SFU Burnaby students quickly find available study spaces on campus.

## Project Overview
Students often struggle to locate quiet and open study areas, especially during exam season.  
CampusConnect helps students view study space availability and plan ahead.

## What I Designed
- Interactive mobile prototype in Figma  
- User journey mapping and wireframes  
- High-fidelity UI screens focused on simplicity and usability

## Tools Used
- Figma  
- UX Design Principles  
- Wireframing & Prototyping
